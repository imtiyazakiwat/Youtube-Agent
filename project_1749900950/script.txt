INT. COMMAND CENTER - NIGHT

将军 (General) paces, eyes on a map. 灯光 (Lights) flicker. 心跳声 (Heartbeats) echo.

将军: (into radio) 部队，准备行动。我们的时间到了。我们必须突破敌人的防线，夺回城市。每个人都要保持警惕，生死在此一搏。

无线电静默。镜头拉远，展现外面的战备景象，士兵们静待命令，夜空中星光与战火交织。

Translation:
General paces, eyes on a map. Lights flicker. Heartbeats echo.

General: (into radio) Troops, get ready. Our time has come. We must break through the enemy’s lines and reclaim the city. Everyone, stay alert; life and death hang in the balance.

Radio silence. The camera pulls back, showing the preparation outside, soldiers awaiting orders, starlight mingling with the fires of war in the night sky.
